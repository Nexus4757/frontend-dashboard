Project Information

Project Title: IoT-enabled, a hyper-local stormwater living laboratory at the University of Maryland

Funded By: UMD Sustainability Fund

In July 2020, three UMD priority stormwater drainage outfalls (#3, #5, #19) were outfitted with fully remote IoT stormwater sensors measuring water quantity and water quality indicators. These measurements help us understand stormwater behavior on campus and support sustainability planning.

PIs: Marccus Hendricks, Priscila Alves, Jason Baer, Elizabeth Bonsignore, Jen Cotting, Tammy Clegg, Allen Davis, Andy Fellows, Mitch Pavao-Zuckerman, Karen Petroff, Qianyao Si, Michael Williams.

Precipitation Data Source: NOAA Daily Precipitation Dataset (Year included in the file)
